Welcome to

Hyde and Lawson Middle Level!

By Cupertino High School Middle Level Committee

Compete

As an FBLA-Middle Level Member, you have the opportunity to participate in multiple FBLA conferences, where you compete in a competition of your choice. You can even go all the way to the National Leadership Conference. Competitions are great ways to have fun while expanding your business knowledge, public speaking skills, and leadership capability.

Make connections

FBLA will give you the chance to meet lots of new people, including students, teachers, and business leaders in their industries. At the various events that FBLA hosts, you will be able to network with others and make friends, connections, and acquaintances!

Have fun!

Most importantly, FBLA is just really FUN! You get to participate in hands-on activities and programs, listen to important business speakers and CEOs speak at FBLA meetings, go on business tours of massive companies, and travel and compete with your friends at conferences!
